# Resume Match Pro - Business Plan (v1)

## 1) Problem

Job seekers lose time because:

- They do not know which jobs to prioritize.
- They struggle to present real skills strongly on resumes.
- Generic job sites do not provide role-personalized coaching.

## 2) Unique Value Proposition

"Career-aware matching + truthful resume coaching."

The app does not only score resume vs JD. It also:

- includes profession correlation (Java/Web/AI/Accounting),
- includes user growth skills (learning/exposure/hands-on/expert),
- outputs effort-aware action guidance (High/Medium/Low match),
- generates tailored, truthful resume actions.

## 3) Ideal Users

- Mid/senior professionals switching jobs.
- Career transition users (newly learned skills, limited confidence in resume writing).
- Students/new grads with project-based exposure.

## 4) Product Scope

### Free

- Manual JD paste
- Resume-to-JD scoring
- Profession + skill-growth correlation
- Tailoring action suggestions

### Pro (subscription)

- Email alert ingestion workflow
- Saved history and pipeline tracking
- Priority notifications for high-match jobs
- Unlimited analyses

## 5) Pricing

- Free: $0
- Pro: $12/month (starter point)
- Team (future): $39/month for multi-profile coaching

## 6) Go-To-Market

1. Launch in developer/job-seeker communities.
2. Publish sample before/after resume improvements.
3. Offer 7-day Pro trial.
4. Measure conversion and retention.

## 7) North Star Metrics

- Weekly active analyzers
- High-match apply rate
- Time-to-apply reduction
- Free-to-pro conversion
- 30-day retention

## 8) Compliance and Trust

- No unauthorized LinkedIn automation.
- Explicit skill-truthfulness guardrails.
- Privacy-first messaging and transparent data handling.

## 9) 30/60/90 Roadmap

### 30 days

- Stabilize scoring and tailoring quality
- Stripe checkout + success/cancel pages
- Basic usage analytics

### 60 days

- Job alert ingestion (email-based)
- Application pipeline board
- Saved profile and versioned resume outputs

### 90 days

- A/B testing of suggestions
- Role-specific templates (backend, AI, accounting, etc.)
- Team and coaching mode
